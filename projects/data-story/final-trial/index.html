In modern society, people are paying more and more attention to health problems, regarding sleep (50%) and exercise (40%) as two of the three main issues that have great impact on their overall health and wellbeing.

This project, thus, starts from National Sleep Foundation's Sleep in America Poll, aiming at revealing a more direct relationship between sleep and exercise via data visualization.
